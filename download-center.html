IMPORTANT NOTICE BEFORE DOWNLOADING TO A MOBILE DEVICE

The download is in the form of a zip file. Most mobile devices do not have the ability to unzip the files or download them in the first place. Unless you are using a download manager app and can save the download files, we highly recommend that you DO NOT use the Download button on mobile devices unless you are sure you can save the PDF, as it will count as one of your downloads. If you want to access the PDF downloads on your mobile device, you MUST download the files via your PC/MAC and transfer them to your mobile device. Download Instructions: This is a simple process

Why only 2 ACTIVATIONS? Each program contains TWO activation periods. We have found that most people do not backup their work. If you backup your files you will never need the second activation period. However we have given you a built-in backup just in case. Once you click the download button, you can download the PDF's as many times as needed for 24 hrs. Once that 24 hr period has expired, clicking the Download button will use another activation period.

  • Click the download button for the program you want to download.
  • Confirm that you want to download your program. Please Note: Program downloads are time stamped and tracked. Downloading the program makes it unrecoverable in the instance of a return and therefore nullifies refund/exchange eligibility.
  • If you are not prompted by your browser to select a file save location, your browser may be set to automatically download .zip files to your default download folder. You can right click on the download button and save the file to a specific path. (The wording of this option in the right click menu is browser specific, it will be something like "Save target as..", "Save link as.." etc...)
  • If your browser does not download the file, you probably have an aggressive antivirus program. Please temporarily disable your antivirus and popup blocker programs and download your file.
